What's an Email Coupon Flyer?

Each week we send 10 coupons to people right here in the {{ current_site.region }}. Coupons for your business can be included in Email Flyers sent to registered consumers who have requested local offers.

Reserved positions in these Email Flyers can be purchased from your Local Business Account. See an Email Flyer.



What's a text message coupon?

Text coupons are short electronic versions of printed coupons. If available, a consumer may send a text coupon to their phone. We offer this option because there's a whole world of people out there that will carry their phone sooner than a fistful of paper coupons. This option allows them to arrive at your cash register with a coupon stored on their phone. If you provide this option, you'll surely get better response. If you're unsure, we recommend you first try including text coupons. You can turn that option off at anytime.
