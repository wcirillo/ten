I no longer want to make a coupon available to website users. How do I remove it?

It's Easy! To stop displaying any current coupon, just turn the "Display" button for that coupon "OFF," (from your account.) Any coupons with the "Display" button set to "ON" will display through the expiration date you choose. Changing the display status of any one coupon does not affect your billing.
