How do I stop getting email or text messages?

You can cancel email instantly. Use the "unsubscribe" link found on our email messages or any page of the website. Text "stop" to 71010 and we'll stop sending text messages. We're always working to improve. Change your mind? We'll welcome you back with open arms.

I signed up, why am I not receiving an Email Flyer?

The Email Flyer is sent Thursdays. Sometimes, email messages get filtered by the email program or internet service provider (ISP). Filtering is meant to discourage SPAM and unwanted messages. Often messages you want get blocked. Sometimes important email is mistakenly sent to folders called “Spam” or “Junk.” We can only guess why certain messages are blocked. Most systems allow you to indicate that a message is NOT SPAM or NOT JUNK. Add Coupons@10coupons.com as a contact to prevent missing any coupons.

Do I see coupons for national online businesses?

Yes. Sometimes valuable national or online coupons are available. However, we'll always provide as many local coupons as possible.
