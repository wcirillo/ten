Location Description

Definitely not required! (Useful in certain situations.)

Help customers to find you and redeem this coupon. A Location Description can be used along with a traditional street address, or instead of one.

Cross streets can be helpful when added to the street address:
Between 33rd and 34th Streets

A familiar location can have an unfamiliar street address. Just say where you are:
3500 Highway 6   Upper Level Fresh Meadows Galleria

You may choose to describe several places with a simple phrase:
"Find our kiosk at all Metro Train Stops."

If you have included a Street Address, a Location Description will display under it.
