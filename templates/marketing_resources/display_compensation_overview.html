Independent Advertising Representative Overview

This is not multi-level marketing. Compensation is commission-based. There is no financial investment.

Your success is based on three areas:

  • Advertising Sales - You provide local business owners with a tool to increase traffic and profits by publishing online coupons.
  • Increasing the Audience of Local Customers - Promote FREE consumer subscriptions to receive valuable local coupons distributed by email.
  • Recruiting Advertising Representatives - Recruit and help train other Independent Advertising Representatives.

Your personalized {{ current_site.domain }} website:

  • A local business operator can purchase a discounted 10 Coupon Publishing Package through the personalized website we supply to you as an Advertising Representative.
  • Purchases occur when you consult with local businesses in person, or on the phone, or anytime a visit to your website results in a purchase.
  • Additional purchases benefit you when the home office contacts your local business advertisers and implements up-sells like our Coupon Email Flyer.
